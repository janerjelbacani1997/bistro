COCKTAIL

/ˈkäkˌtāl/

Cocktails are great for alcoholic drinkers! It consists of a spirit mixture or several spirit mixed with other ingredients, which will give a taste of satisfaction.


Margarita


Ingredients:

  • Don Julio Blanco Tequila
  • Grand Marnier
  • Lime Juice

Procedure:

  • Fill a shaker with ice
  • Add Grand Marnier Cordon Rouge Liquer, Don Julio Blanco Tequila and Lime to the shaker.
  • Shake until cold
  • Strain into a short glass

Martini


Ingredients:

  • Gordon's London Dry Gin
  • Dry Vermouth
  • Olive

Procedure:

  • Fill a shaker with ice
  • Add Gordon's London Dry Gin and dry vermouth to the shaker
  • Shake until cold
  • Strain into a martini glass
  • Garnish with an olive

Manhattan


Ingredients:

  • The Singleton Single Malt
  • Sweet Vermouth
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Orange
  • Maraschino Cherry

Procedure:

  • Place The Singleton of Dufftown Malt Whisky, sweet vermouth and angostura bitters into a mixing glass.
  • Fill the glass with ice, stir the mixture until well combined and strain into a martini glass.
  • Garnish with an orange twist and maraschino cherry.

Mojito


Ingredients:

  • Captain Morgan® White Rum
  • Soda Water
  • Caster Sugar
  • Lime Wedge
  • Mint Sprig

Procedure:

  • Muddle sugar and lime wedges together in a glass.
  • Muddle mint.
  • Add ice to a glass.
  • Add Captain Morgan White Rum® and soda.
  • Stir with a spoon.
  • Add ice and a sprig of mint.

Old Fashioned


Ingredients:

  • Johnnie Walker Black Label
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Sugar
  • Cherry
  • Orange

Procedure:

  • Place a sugar cube in a glass and add a dash of angostura bitters.
  • Muddle until dissolved.
  • Fill the glass with ice.
  • Pour in Johnnie Walker Black Label Blended Scotch Whisky.
  • Garnish with a cherry and orange slice.

Piña Colada


Ingredients:

  • Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold
  • Coconut Cream<
  • Pineapple Chunks
  • Cherry to Garnish

Procedure:

  • Put a large hand full of crushed ice into your blender.
  • Add the Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold, coconut cream and pineapple chunks into the blender.

Strawberry Daiquiri


Ingredients:

  • Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Sugar Syrup
  • Fresh Chopped Strawberries

Procedure:

  • Fill your shaker 3/4 full with cubed ice.
  • Pour in Captain Morgan Original Spiced Gold, sugar syrup, fresh lime juice and 3 chopped strawberries.
  • Shake vigorously for 10 seconds until cold.
  • Strain into a martini glass and garnish with a strawberry.

Sazerac


Ingredients:

  • Bulleit Bourbon
  • Sugar
  • Peychaud's Bitters
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Absinthe
  • Lemon Peel

Procedure:

  • Fill a short glass with ice.
  • Top up with water and leave to chill.
  • Place a sugar cube in a new glass and add bitters.
  • Muddle until dissolved.
  • Add two or three ice cubes.
  • Pour in Bulleit Bourbon.
  • Stir well.
  • Discard the ice and water from the first glass.
  • Rinse the glass with absinthe.
  • Strain the cocktail into the glass. Do not add ice.
  • Garnish with a strip of lemon peel zest.
